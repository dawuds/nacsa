### Appendix L — Glossary and Definitions

| Term | Definition | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Act 854** | Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854), the primary cyber security legislation in Malaysia administered by NACSA. | Cyber Security Act 2024 |
| **CoP** | Code of Practice — a code issued by NACSA under Act 854 specifying technical or procedural requirements for NCII entities. | Act 854 |
| **CISO** | Chief Information Security Officer — the senior executive responsible for the Organization's information and cyber security programme. | Internal |
| **CSO** | Cyber Security Officer — the officer designated pursuant to Act 854 as responsible for the Organization's cyber security obligations. | Act 854 |
| **MTTD** | Mean Time to Detect — the average time between the occurrence of a cyber security incident and its detection by the Organization. | Industry Standard |
| **MTTR** | Mean Time to Respond — the average time between detection of an incident and the commencement of containment actions. | Industry Standard |
| **MTTRec** | Mean Time to Recover — the average time between detection of an incident and full restoration of affected systems. | Industry Standard |
| **NACSA** | National Cyber Security Agency — the Malaysian government agency responsible for overseeing national cyber security under Act 854. | Act 854 |
| **NCII** | National Critical Information Infrastructure — information infrastructure designated under Act 854 as critical to national security, public safety, economy, or the provision of essential services. | Act 854 |
| **NCII Entity** | An entity designated by NACSA as owning, operating, or providing a service in relation to NCII. | Act 854 |
| **PIR** | Post-Incident Review — a structured review conducted following a cyber security incident to identify root causes and lessons learned. | Internal |
| **RACI** | Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed — a framework for defining roles and responsibilities. | Industry Standard |
| **RMiT** | Risk Management in Technology — a Bank Negara Malaysia policy document governing technology risk management for financial institutions. | BNM |
| **s18** | Section 18 of the Cyber Security Act 2024 — compliance declaration obligations. | Act 854 |
| **s21** | Section 21 of the Cyber Security Act 2024 — mandatory incident reporting obligations. | Act 854 |
| **s22** | Section 22 of the Cyber Security Act 2024 — risk assessment, audit, and cyber security management obligations. | Act 854 |
| **SOC** | Security Operations Centre — the function responsible for continuous monitoring and detection of cyber security events. | Internal |
