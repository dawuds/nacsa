## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This report presents the findings of the annual evaluation of [Organisation Name]'s Cyber Security Awareness Programme for the reporting period [DD Month YYYY] to [DD Month YYYY]. The purpose of this report is to:

- Measure the effectiveness of awareness training delivered across the organisation;
- Assess staff knowledge retention, behavioural change, and security culture maturity;
- Identify gaps in awareness and areas requiring targeted remediation;
- Inform the planning and improvement of the next cycle of the awareness programme; and
- Demonstrate compliance with the **Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854), Section 18**, which requires licensed entities to implement and maintain cyber security awareness and training programmes for personnel.

### 1.3 Scope

This report covers all personnel employed by [Organisation Name] during the reporting period, including:

- **Full-time employees** across all business units and departments
- **Contract and third-party staff** with access to [Organisation Name]'s information systems or data
