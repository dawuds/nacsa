### 2.3 Key Obligations Under Act 854 — Section 18

Section 18 of the Cyber Security Act 2024 imposes the following primary obligations on NCII entities, which this report addresses:

- **s18(1):** Duty to comply with the Code of Practice issued or approved by NACSA.
- **s18(2):** Duty to comply with directives issued by NACSA or the relevant Sector Lead Agency.
- **s18(3):** Duty to take all reasonable steps to manage and reduce cyber security risk to NCII.
- **s18(4):** Duty to report cyber security incidents to NACSA within prescribed timeframes.
- **s18(5):** Duty to provide information, assistance, and access to NACSA as required.
