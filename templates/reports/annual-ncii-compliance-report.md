## 2. Purpose and Scope

### 2.1 Purpose

This Annual NCII Compliance Report is prepared by [Organization Name] in accordance with the requirements of the **Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854)**, specifically:

- **Section 18** — Obligations of NCII entities in relation to cybersecurity measures, risk assessment, and compliance reporting to NACSA and the sector lead
- **Section 22** — Obligations relating to the notification of cybersecurity incidents affecting NCII

This report serves the following purposes:

1. To demonstrate [Organization Name]'s compliance with all applicable obligations under Act 854 for the reporting period
