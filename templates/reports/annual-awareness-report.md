## 2. Purpose and Scope

### 2.1 Purpose

This Annual Security Awareness Report documents the planning, execution, participation, and outcomes of [Organization Name]'s Security Awareness Programme for the financial year ending **[DD Month YYYY]**. The report serves to:

- Demonstrate compliance with statutory obligations under the **Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854)**, specifically Section 18, which mandates that designated entities implement measures to promote cybersecurity awareness among their personnel;
- Provide the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), Senior Management, and the Board Risk Committee with a factual basis to assess the maturity and effectiveness of the organization's human-layer security controls;
- Identify gaps in staff security knowledge and behaviour, and inform the design of the following year's programme;
- Serve as an auditable record for regulatory examination by the **National Cyber Security Agency (NACSA)** and any other competent authority.

### 2.2 Scope

This report covers all security awareness activities conducted by or on behalf of [Organization Name] during the period **[DD Month YYYY] to [DD Month YYYY]**, including:

- **In-scope entities and personnel:** All permanent employees, contract staff, third-party vendors with system access, and Board members of [Organization Name] and its subsidiaries as listed in Appendix A.

- **Excluded:** [Describe any exclusions, e.g., staff on extended leave, newly onboarded staff in final week of period, specific subsidiaries not yet integrated into the programme.]
