## 1. Purpose and Scope

*This section establishes the intent and boundaries of this register.*

This Standards Compliance Tracker serves as the authoritative register for [Organization Name]'s compliance posture against all cybersecurity standards applicable under the **Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854)**, with particular reference to **Section 24**, which mandates that designated Critical Information Infrastructure (CII) entities comply with standards specified or approved by the National Cyber Security Agency (NACSA).

This register:

- Records every applicable NACSA-specified standard, framework, and technical requirement binding upon [Organization Name] as a CII entity.
- Tracks current compliance status against each requirement, identifies gaps, and documents remediation activities.
- Provides a consolidated evidence base for regulatory examination, internal audit, and senior management reporting.
- Is reviewed on a **quarterly** basis or immediately following any material change to applicable standards, the organization's CII scope, or a significant cybersecurity incident.

## 10. Escalation and Reporting

*This section defines when and how compliance status must be escalated.*

### 10.1 Escalation Triggers

The following conditions require immediate escalation to the Board Risk Committee and, where applicable, notification to NACSA:

- Any **Critical** severity gap identified
- Any remediation plan overdue by more than **30 days**
- Any reduction in overall compliance posture from one quarter to the next
- Any new NACSA directive that introduces previously untracked requirements
- Any regulatory examination or inquiry relating to Act 854 s24 compliance

### 10.2 Reporting Schedule

| Report | Audience | Frequency | Prepared By | Approved By |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Standards Compliance Summary | Board Risk Committee | Quarterly | CISO | Board Risk Chair |
