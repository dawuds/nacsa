## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This Training Records Register documents the completion of cyber security training programmes and competency assessments for all personnel with access to National Critical Information Infrastructure (NCII) assets operated by [Organization Name]. It serves as the primary evidence register for demonstrating compliance with training obligations under the **Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854)**, specifically **Section 18**, which imposes a duty on NCII sector leads and NCII entities to ensure that personnel responsible for managing and operating designated NCII are adequately trained and competent in cyber security.

This register is a **mandatory, continuously maintained record** and constitutes primary audit evidence during NACSA inspections, regulatory assessments, and internal audits.

### 1.2 Scope

This register applies to:

- All employees, contractors, and third-party personnel holding access privileges to [Organization Name]'s designated NCII systems, networks, or data
- All personnel fulfilling roles defined under the organization's NCII responsibility framework
- Training programmes mandated by NACSA, [Organization Name]'s CISO, and applicable sector-specific requirements under Act 854
- All organizational units within [Organization Name] operating under the NCII designation: [List NCII-designated systems/units]

### 1.3 Regulatory Basis

| Instrument | Provision | Obligation |
|---|---|---|
| Cyber Security Act 2024 (Act 854) | Section 18 | Duty to ensure cyber security of NCII; competency of responsible personnel |
| NACSA NCII Sector Guidelines | [Applicable Sector Guideline Reference] | Sector-specific training requirements |
| [Organization Name] Cyber Security Policy | [Policy Reference] | Internal training mandate and minimum standards |
| Personal Data Protection Act 2010 | [Applicable Section] | Data handling competency for personnel with access to personal data within NCII |
